Kura Sushi

What makes Kura special is that they have a conveyor belt next to each seat. Customers can see the actual food before they order. If they want to order anything, they just need to take the food out of the belt any time. There is no need to call the waiters which can save a lot of time. My favorite dishes are salmon sushi and fried squid.
