HaiDiLao Hot Pot

Hot Pot is a traditional and very popular food in Chinese culture. Every one sits around the table and cook their food in a large boiled hot pot. There are many hot pot resturants, HaiDiLao is one of my favorites. They provide fresh, variety meat, vegetable and seafood. They also provide the best services.

Their Sevices

I like HaiDiLao because they provide the best services among all other resturants. They provide chest, cards, snacks, fruits and drinks for customers who are waiting to be seated. They even have their own nail salon and massage service provide to their customers for free.
